# WEBSITE MAINTENANCE SERVICES PROPOSAL

**Prepared for:** MARBOYS Billiard POS System  
**Prepared by:** Joshua Fronda  
**Date:** April 16, 2026

---

## 1. Executive Summary

This proposal outlines the terms and scope of ongoing website maintenance services for the **MARBOYS Billiard POS System**, developed using React, Node.js, and SQLite. The objective of this engagement is to ensure the system remains operational, secure, updated, and aligned with the business operations of MARBOYS.

Ongoing technical oversight is essential to maintain performance, reliability, and professional presentation of the point-of-sale system.

---

## 2. Scope of Services

The following services are included under the Monthly Website Maintenance Plan:

### 2.1 Content Management
- Updating menu items, prices, and categories
- Modifying table rates and exhibition match settings
- Adding or updating food and beverage items
- Up to **10 minor content revisions per month**

### 2.2 Technical Maintenance
- Website rebuild and deployment management
- Routine system checks to ensure site availability
- Troubleshooting minor technical issues (receipt printing, calculations, reports)
- Coordination with hosting provider when necessary
- Database backups and optimization

### 2.3 Bug Fixes & Issue Resolution
- Fixing calculation errors in sales totals
- Resolving PDF download and display issues
- Correcting time display and timezone issues
- Addressing user interface glitches

### 2.4 Feature Adjustments
- Minor modifications to existing features (exhibition fees, visibility toggles, etc.)
- Small UI/UX improvements
- Role-based access adjustments (Owner/Admin/Cashier)

### 2.5 Performance Monitoring
- Cross-device and browser compatibility checks
- Basic performance optimization
- Monitoring for broken links or display inconsistencies
- Receipt and report generation testing

---

## 3. Services Not Included

The following services are outside the scope of this agreement and will be subject to separate quotation and approval:

- Major redesign or structural changes
- Development of new modules (inventory, payroll, accounting)
- Integration with external payment gateways
- E-commerce implementation for online orders
- Mobile app development
- Advanced analytics or AI features
- Multi-branch or franchise system expansion
